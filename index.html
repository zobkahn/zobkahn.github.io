I am a seventh year PhD student at the UC Berkeley School of Information co-advised by Jenna Burrell and Joshua Blumenstock. My research informs the design of more responsible technology and tech policy by leveraging insights of people with lived expertise, alongside those of people with domain expertise (e.g., data scientists, policymaker, privacy scholars).

At UC Berkeley, I am affiliated with the Global Policy Lab, AI Policy Hub, and Algorithmic Fairness and Opacity Group.
